# Snoop / 思脑谱

> A changelog of a human mind.

## English

Snoop is a long-term personal archive of thoughts, memories, creative works, and observations.

It exists to preserve how a person experiences the world, remembers what has passed, and develops ideas over time. This repository is not a final theory of consciousness. It is an ongoing record of a human mind changing through time.

### How to read

Entries are kept in `log/`:

- `notes/` — raw thoughts, observations, and thinking in progress
- `essays/` — structured arguments and longer reflections
- `prose/` — prose, nonfiction, and literary writing
- `poems/` — poems and lyrics
- `lost-world/` — places, people, and worlds that no longer exist in reality but remain in memory

Files generally follow `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`. Older works without an exact date may use `YYYY-title.md`. Read chronologically to follow the changelog, or browse by form and subject. Earlier entries remain part of the archive; new understanding is added rather than written over them.

## 中文

思脑谱是一个长期维护的人类意识档案，记录创作、记忆、观察和思考过程。

它保存一个人在时间中的变化：如何感知世界，如何保存记忆，如何形成观点，以及如何不断修正自己。它不是关于意识的最终答案，而是一份持续更新的个人记录。

### 如何阅读

所有记录保存在 `log/` 中：

- `notes/` — 原始想法、观察记录与思维过程
- `essays/` — 有明确结构的文章、分析与长篇思考
- `prose/` — 散文、非虚构叙事与文学性文本
- `poems/` — 诗歌与歌词
- `lost-world/` — 现实中已经不存在、但仍存在于记忆中的人、空间与世界

文件通常使用 `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` 命名；没有准确日期的旧作可以使用 `YYYY-title.md`。可以按时间阅读，观察这份记录如何演变；也可以按文体与主题浏览。旧记录不会被新的理解覆盖，变化本身就是档案的一部分。
