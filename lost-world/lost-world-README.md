# Lost World

Lost World is an archive of worlds that once existed in reality but can no longer be returned to.

These are not simple memories.

They are preserved scenes:
places, people, objects, sounds, smells, and moments that formed a complete world at a certain point in time.

A Lost World may disappear because of:

- the passing of people;
- the change or disappearance of places;
- the end of relationships;
- the irreversible movement of time.

The purpose of this archive is not to reconstruct history with perfect accuracy.

It is to preserve the experience of a world that once existed.

Each record is a snapshot of a reality that was once lived.

# 消逝世界

Lost World 记录那些曾经真实存在，但现实中已经无法再次进入的世界。

它不是普通的回忆。

它保存的是一个完整的场景：

其中包含：

- 地点；
- 人；
- 物品；
- 声音；
- 气味；
- 身体感受；
- 当时的情绪。

一个世界可能因为：

- 人的离去；
- 空间的改变；
- 关系的结束；
- 时间不可逆的流逝；

而无法再次回到。

这个档案并不试图证明过去的每个细节是否完全准确。

它保存的是：

一个人在某个时间点，曾经真实经历过的世界。

每一份记录，都是一个已经无法回返的现实切片。
